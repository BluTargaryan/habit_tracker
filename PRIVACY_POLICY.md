# Privacy Policy for Habit Tracker

**Last updated:** [FILL IN DATE BEFORE PUBLISHING]

This policy explains what information Habit Tracker ("the app") collects, how it's used, and your choices. Habit Tracker is designed to keep your data on your device — there is no account server and no backend database.

## Information We Collect

**Account information you provide:** name, username, age, country, and a password. Your password is hashed before storage and is never stored or transmitted in plain text. This information is stored only in a local database on your device (via sqflite) and is never sent to us or any third party.

**Habit data:** the habits you create, their colors, completion history, and streaks. Stored only in the local on-device database.

**Notification preferences:** which habits you've chosen to receive reminders for and at what times of day. Stored only in local on-device settings.

**Location data:** if you enable weather features for outdoor habits or the Motivation & Weather page, the app requests your device's location (with your explicit permission) to fetch local weather conditions. Your coordinates are sent directly to the free, no-authentication Open-Meteo weather API to retrieve a forecast — they are not stored by us, not linked to your account, and not shared with any other party. You can deny location access; the app remains fully functional without it (you can search for a city manually instead).

## Third-Party Services

The app makes network requests to two external, no-authentication APIs to power optional features:

- **ZenQuotes** (zenquotes.io) — to fetch a motivational quote. No personal data is sent with this request.
- **Open-Meteo** (open-meteo.com) — to fetch weather data for a given location. Only coordinates or a searched city name are sent; no personal or account data is included.

Neither service receives your name, username, password, habit data, or any other information you enter into the app.

## Data Storage & Security

All personal and habit data is stored locally on your device using standard local storage (sqflite for structured data, device preferences for settings). We do not operate a server, do not collect analytics, and do not have access to your data. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted with it.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data lives on your device, you control it directly: editing or deleting a habit, or uninstalling the app, permanently removes the corresponding data. There is no remote copy to separately request deletion of.

## Children's Privacy

Habit Tracker requires users to be at least 16 years old to register and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
