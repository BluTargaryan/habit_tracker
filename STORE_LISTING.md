## Full description (4000 char max — currently ~1,650)
```
Habit Tracker helps you build consistency, one day at a time. Add the habits that matter to you, check them off with a swipe, and watch your streaks grow.

WHY HABIT TRACKER

• Simple daily view — your homepage splits habits into "To Do" and "Completed," so you always know what's left today. Swipe a card to mark it done (or undo it) without leaving the screen.

• Streaks that keep you honest — every habit tracks its current streak and longest streak, so you can see your consistency at a glance and stay motivated to keep it going.

• Full history — open any habit to see a day-by-day record of what you completed and what you missed.

• Reports & charts — a weekly summary and visual chart show your completion patterns across all your habits, with a filter to focus on what's done or still outstanding.

• Personalize your habits — pick a color for each one so your lists are easy to scan at a glance.

• Reminders on your schedule — turn on notifications for just the habits you're actively working on, and choose morning, afternoon, and/or evening reminder times.

• Daily motivation — a fresh motivational quote every day, refreshable on demand, right on your homepage.

• Weather, built in — check today's forecast without leaving the app, and see a quick weather snippet on any habit you've marked as outdoor, so you know before you head out.

• Your data stays yours — everything is stored securely on your device. No ads, no data selling, no account required beyond a simple local sign-in.

Whether you're building a fitness routine, a reading habit, or just trying to drink more water, Habit Tracker keeps it simple: see what's left today, mark it done, and watch your streak grow.
```
